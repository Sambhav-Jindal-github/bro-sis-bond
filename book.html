Teri saathiyon mein meri khushiyan chhupi hui hain, aur teri bholi si muskurahat mere dil ko hamesha khush rakhti hai.

Ek mulaqat, ek pal, jisse main hamesha yaad rakhunga. Vo din, jab hum do behene ek doosre se pehli baar mile, mere dil ko choo gaya. Tujhe dekh kar mujhe ek alag hi khushi mili, jaise koi apna koi khooya hua rishta dobara mil gaya ho.

Vo chand lamhe, jo hamare saath bitaaye gaye, vo ek choti si baatein, ek doosre ko samajhne ki koshish - sab kuch mere liye bahut khaas hai. Tere chehre par vo muskurahat, jo mere dil ko hamesha yaad rahegi. Tere bina meri duniya adhoori si hai, lekin vo pal jo humne ek saath bitaya, woh mere dil mein hamesha jinda rahega.

Tere saath bitaaye vo kuch hi palon mein maine tujhe apni behen maan liya. Tere pyaar aur tere saath bitaye vo lamhe ne mere dil ko chhoo liya. Har baar, jab main tujhe yaad karta hoon, mujhe vo pal yaad aata hai jab hum do behene ek doosre ke saath the, muskurahat mein bhi pyaar aur ek dusre ki parwah thi.

Hamari mulaqat ke baad, har ek call, har ek message ne mere zindagi ko roshan kar diya hai. Tere saath ki hui vo ek mulaqat ne mujhe sikha diya hai ki rishte dil se judte hain, jo dooriyan ko paar kar sakte hain. Tere bina humne shayad sirf ek baar hi mulaqat ki ho, lekin teri yaadon ne mere dil ko hamesha gunguna liya hai.

Vo din jab hum milne gaye the, vo yaadon ka ek khubsurat chhaya mere dil mein bana hua hai. Teri awaaz, teri khushi, vo saath mein bitaye kuch hi lamhe - sab mere liye bahut maayne rakhte hain. Tere saath mein bitaya vo samay mere liye ek anmol tohfa hai, jo main hamesha apne dil mein saath rakhunga.

Tere bina toh humne sirf ek doosre ko virtual duniya mein hi dekha hai, lekin tere dil se juda hokar, tere pyaar ne mujhe apna bana liya hai. Tujhe paane ka yeh ek mauka mere liye bahut khaas tha, aur main hamesha is pal ko apne dil mein saath rakhunga.
